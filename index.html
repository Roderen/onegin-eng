About the cottage village

A unique project for the Tyumen region. By 2030, we plan to implement 10 phases with 180 sections on average in each phase.

On sale are plots for construction according to their own design and plots with finished houses. In both cases, the classic architectural style in the beige and chocolate palette of exterior decoration serves as a design code for the entire village as a whole.

2 400 plots Plots ranging from 8 to 20 acres can be chosen near the forest or closer to the road
15 minutes from the city A daily trip to the city along the most picturesque highway in Tyumen will become a pleasant routine
Plots from 710,000 $ All inclusive. The plots have already been supplied with gas, electricity, roads and even the Internet.
Convenient payment methods Mortgages, installments, and a 4% discount for full prepayment. Other conditions can be discussed individually
3 purchase options You can choose a plot with a finished house, with a contract or a plot without development, for example, for the future
Closed area An entrance group with security and barriers will protect residents from uninvited guests
